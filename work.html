TravelCrumbs

TravelCrumbs helps people spend their money while traveling.

It shows the exchange rates of the current location while it keeps tracking the locations using GPS. As you travel from country to country, all you need to do is to set your currency in your own country. And wherever you are in, TravelCrumbs will detect your location and automatically change the currency. In case you travel domestically TravelCrumbs will be still helpful for tracking where you have visited and checking the amount of money you spend each time.

Users can easily input via voice control and check the price directly. They can also see an overview of their total expenditure for the day and the places that they have visited.

Currently, we support English and Spanish voice input. If you want your country's language pack, please contact me. TravelCrumbs is able to detect any value that you have said. We have the accurate exchange rates and location datas for over 180 countries worldwide. TravelCrumbs uses Openexchangerates's and Google's geocoding API.
